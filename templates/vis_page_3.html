City Latitude vs Cloudiness

This plot shows the relationship between the degree of latitude of a city and the maximum temperature the city will reach on a given day. From this Visualization we can see a trend that tempearture is the highest at the 0 degree latitude, and that as you move to either side, the remperature tends to decrease.
